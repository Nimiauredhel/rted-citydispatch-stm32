Detailed Description

Implements the "Event Tracker" structure, where pending events are stored, until they reach a final outcome state (handled, failed, or otherwise dismissed). It is necessary since a single event contains a multitude of associated jobs that often require multiple agents from different departments to handle, which means that an event cannot be simply passed on to a singular department or agent.
